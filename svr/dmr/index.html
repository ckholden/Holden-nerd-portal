Hotspot → PNW connection (RF use, not the monitor)
Connects your hotspot to PNWDigital so your radio can work PNW through it. This is separate from the PNW monitor below — the monitor always watches the whole network no matter this switch. Off by default: PNW and TGIF share a timeslot, so leaving both on lets PNW silently bump HOLDEN1. Turn on only when traveling out of PNW repeater range.
🌐 PNW = whole-network CallWatch (~8 sec) · 🏠 Hotspot = your Pi-Star last-heard (~5 sec). Each source shows its own clock.
